Before you submit

Make sure your paper is ready for basic screening. This helps us process your submission faster.

  • Check grammar, spelling and basic structure (introduction, methodology, results, conclusion).
  • Ensure plagiarism is within reasonable academic limits as per your college norms.
  • Confirm all author names are spelled correctly and appear in the right order.
  • Keep your file name clear, e.g., “Name_College_PaperTitle.pdf”.
What happens next?
  • You'll receive a confirmation email with your submission details.
  • Our editorial team reviews your paper and shares a decision (accept / revise / reject).
  • On acceptance, you'll receive payment details (₹300 basic publication fee).
  • After publication, e-certificates are shared with all authors.
